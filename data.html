About this data

Resort Atlas maps Maldives operating resort records and resort development records against OneMap island geometry, then cross-checks both against the Paradise Leased / MMPRC island lease spreadsheet. The datasets below are the raw inputs and the derived outputs powering the map. Every file is downloadable for your own GIS or analysis work.

Datasets

Operating resorts

—

Operating resort records sourced from the Ministry / CSV list. Each record keeps the original source status separately from a cross-checked verified status, plus rooms, beds, operator, owner/lessee, and evidence links where a status was corrected.

Operating resorts CSV

Resorts under development

—

Resort development sites from the Ministry of Tourism "under development" list (07 April 2025), including project type, lessee/holder, source PDF page, and any explicit coordinates extracted from the document.

Development resorts CSV Explicit coordinates GeoJSON

Combined join table

—

Operating and development records merged into one flat table formatted for joining against OneMap island geometry in a GIS. This is the convenience export for spatial analysis.

Combined for OneMap join CSV

Status verification

—

The records where the verified status differs from the source status, with confidence level and the independent evidence sources used. These corrections are highlighted on the map.

Status corrections CSV

MMPRC cross-match

—

The output of cross-matching resort records against the Paradise Leased spreadsheet: which islands are implicated in the MMPRC lease scheme, the lease holder, acquisition cost, and the method used to make each match.

MMPRC cross-match CSV

Paradise Leased source

—

The source island-lease spreadsheet behind the MMPRC layer. Most rows are implicated in the scheme; a couple are flagged "context only" where the comments note no suggestion of wrongdoing or a lease that predates the scheme.

Paradise Leased islands CSV Paradise Leased islands JSON

Island geometry comes from the public OneMap ArcGIS layer, fetched live by the map and not redistributed here. Status corrections rely on at least two independent sources where possible (official site or booking engine, live listings, recent trade news). Record counts above are read from the live data files.
