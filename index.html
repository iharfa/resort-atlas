About Resort Atlas

Resort Atlas maps operating resort records and resort development records against OneMap island geometry.

What this page shows

  • Operating resort records from the provided CSV, with separate source and verified status fields.
  • Resorts under development from the Ministry of Tourism list updated on 07 April 2025.
  • Explicit coordinate sites extracted from the PDF.
  • OneMap island outlines fetched live from the public ArcGIS layer.
  • A satellite basemap for visual checking against resort islands and lagoons.
  • MMPRC corruption implicated islands cross-matched from the Paradise Leased spreadsheet.

Status validation

  • The app keeps the Ministry or CSV status as source status.
  • Cross-checked records receive a verified status, confidence level, and evidence links.
  • Discrepancies are highlighted on the map and exported in the verification CSV.

Matching logic

  • Atoll and island name exact match first.
  • Unique island name match second.
  • Fuzzy matching inside the same atoll third.
  • Records with no confident match go to the manual review queue.
